Restaurants

should be okay for lunch/dinner

The Traders | Steakhouse

Price: ~200k / person

A pretty expensive restaurant. But it's near to the city, and I have never tried it before. Just don't order the wagyu cut haha.

Hibiki | Japanese

Price: ~125k / person

It's not too bad. I feel like the Palang Merah branch is nicer and prettier, and it's worth a shot again. Not so expensive and the taste is nice.

Palms | Cafe

Price: ~125k / person

It's actually quite good - I can see myself eating here again. And I feel like the atmospehre is not too bad. The portion is okay, and it's quite creative. I like the salted egg and the pasta.

Angry Korean BBQ | Barbeque

Price: ~100-150k / person

Ate here a few times - turns out to be very good. Especially for it's price, and the portion, the meat is properly marinated, tender, we can cook it ourselves to ensure it's cooked - it's so underrated.

JJ Steak | Steak

Price: ~300k / person

One of the best steaks in Medan for real - it's so tender, and the flavor's so right. May be very pricey, but I believe that it's so worth it.

Magal | Barbeque

Price: ~150k / person

Maybe a bit overrated but it's still very good. I've been there a few times and every single time, it's nice.

Mula | Cafe

Price: ~60k / person

It's really not bad, especially for it's price. Although quite small, so if possible we need to go during weekdays. The cheesecake is fantastic. You told me you always wanted to visit here.
